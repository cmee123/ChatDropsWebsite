ChatDrops

ChatDrops is a Google Chrome extension that helps you measure the environmental impact of your AI usage: specifically on ChatGPT. The extension does this by tracking the amount of water your activities consume throughout the day and over time, measured in fluid ounces. To further contextualize this usage, ChatDrops calculates the number of waterbottles and hand washes that your water consumption is equal to. Every time you send a query to ChatGPT, ChatDrops will automatically calculate the impact of your actions and a popup in the corner will show the damage.

Additionally, the UI "fills up" with water as you send more queries to ChatGPT. We recommend a maximum of 20 queries per day, and the water level indicates how close you are to reaching that limit.
